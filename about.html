About New Life Charitable Trust

The New life Charitable Trust (NLCT) is a registered non-governmental, secular, non profitable, unpaid social service organization established in 1991 with an objective to serve two cross sections of society – The destitute and abandoned elderly people and Orphaned and abandoned children.

Vision

Selfless service and charity to the poor to make them self-sustainable.


Mission

Ensuring a life of dignity for destitute adults, abandoned senior citizens, orphaned and helpless children and Caring for cattle


Our Values
  • Respect for all
  • Transparency
  • Inclusion
  • Equal treatment for all
  • Compassion

The Seed and The Beginning

The seed for NEW LIFE was sown in 1991 at ERAIYUR village by Mr.A.A.Swamy and Mrs.G.Lalitha, who took VRS from their respective Govt. jobs to start their philanthropic services for the betterment of distressed Sr.Citizens and destitute parentless Children. The seed has grown into a big banyan tree over a period of 26 years, providing Charity Care to those neglected by the society, with the help and sustained support of many well wishers and philanthropists.
